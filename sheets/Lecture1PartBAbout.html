About Walnut Avenue's BBB





Our mission.

We believe that every visitor of Roxbury's great Walnut avenue, has a fundamental right to a good beer, a gilded bed, and a generous breakfast.


Our Story.

Hailing from humble beginnings, our inn, was no better than any other old, roach infested 50 year building, that could not meet its maker due to regulations from the city. Slowly, and yes we mean slowly, We have restored it back to it's former glory. The newly refurbished Walnut Avenue's BBB, comes with state of the art facilities, at the affordable price range that american tourists, desperately need.
